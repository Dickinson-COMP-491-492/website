## Assigned Readings

1. [Introduction to "A Framework for Ethical Decision-Making"](https://www.scu.edu/ethics/ethics-resources/ethical-decision-making/introduction-to-a-framework-for-ethical-decision-making/) by Jonathan Kwan, Margaret McLean, Irina Raicu from the Santa Clara Markkula Center for Applied Ethics.
2. [A Framework for Ethical Decision Making](https://www.scu.edu/ethics/ethics-resources/a-framework-for-ethical-decision-making/)from the Santa Clara Markkula Center for Applied Ethics.
3. [Proactive CARE for Computing Professionals](https://www.acm.org/binaries/content/assets/code-of-ethics/proactive-care-for-computing-professionals.pdf) from the ACM Committee on Professional Ethics.
